Privacy Policy for NETD4TE

Last Updated: [Date of Last Update]

Welcome to NETD4TE! Your privacy is a top priority for us. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your personal data when you use our application (the "Service").

1. Our Privacy-First Philosophy

Before we get into the details, we want to highlight our core commitments to your privacy:

  • We Do Not Track Your Precise Location. Unlike many other apps, we do not request or track your device's GPS data. Our "Nearby" feature works with keywords, not coordinates, to give you full control.
  • We Do Not Access Your Camera. We will never ask for permission to access your device's camera.
  • We Have Minimal Access to Your Photos. We only ask for permission to access the specific photos you choose to upload to your profile. We cannot see or access any other photos on your device.

2. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our Service, we collect the following types of information:

A. Information You Provide to Us Directly

When you create an account, you provide us with certain information that is necessary for the Service to work. This includes:

  • Account Credentials: Your email address and a password to secure your account.
  • Profile Information: Your public-facing profile name, username, bio, date of birth, gender, and country.
  • Photos: The profile picture and three swipe images you choose to upload.
  • Optional Information: Your Instagram username, should you choose to provide it.

B. Permissions We Request

To enable certain features, we request your permission to access the following:

  • Photo Library Access: To allow you to select and upload photos for your profile.
  • Notifications: To send you alerts about new messages, matches, and other app-related activities. You can manage this in your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account and provide you with our Service.
  • To display your profile to other users.
  • To enable communication between users who have matched.
  • To operate and improve our Service, including developing new features.
  • To protect the safety and security of our users by preventing and addressing fraudulent or illegal activities.
  • To provide you with customer support.
  • To show relevant third-party advertisements (e.g., from Google AdMob) that allow us to offer certain features for free.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your public profile information is visible to other users of the Service.
  • With Ad Partners: We partner with ad providers, such as Google AdMob and potentially others in the future, who may collect information to serve personalized ads. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to comply with legal processes, protect our rights, or ensure the safety of our users.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You have full control over your personal information:

  • Access and Update: You can review and edit your profile information at any time within the app's settings.
  • Account Deletion: You can permanently delete your account at any time using the "Delete Account" feature in the app. When you delete your account, all of your data is immediately and permanently removed from our servers. There is no recovery period.

6. Our Future Plans

We are constantly working to enhance NETD4TE. In the future, we plan to introduce premium memberships and the ability to purchase virtual currency with real money. If and when these features are introduced, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect any new data processing practices.

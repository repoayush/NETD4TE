Monetization & Features

Learn about NETD4TE's in-app economy and the exciting features you can unlock.

1. Our Virtual Currencies

NETD4TE uses two types of virtual currency to access special features:

  • Gold: Used for standard premium actions, like sending "Introvert Requests".
  • Diamonds: Used for more exclusive features and premium interactions.

You can always see your current balance of Gold and Diamonds in the app's home screen app bar.

2. The Token Screen: Your Reward Hub

To access your rewards, simply tap on your Gold or Diamond balance from the home screen. This will take you to the Token Screen, where you can claim all your rewards.

3. How to Earn Currency (Current Version)

In the current version of NETD4TE, all rewards are earned by watching short video ads. This allows us to provide premium features to everyone.

  • Daily Rewards: Log in every day to claim an increasing amount of Gold!
    • Day 1: 100 Gold
    • Day 2: 125 Gold
    • ...and so on, with a 25 Gold increase each consecutive day for a week!
  • Emergency Gold: Need a little extra? You can claim 15 Gold by watching an ad, an unlimited number of times.
  • Claim Diamonds: You can also claim 5 Diamonds by watching an ad.

4. Future Plans: Premium Subscriptions & Direct Purchases

We are constantly working to improve your experience. In an upcoming update, we will introduce more ways to enjoy NETD4TE's premium features:

  • Direct Purchases: You will be able to buy Gold and Diamond packs directly using secure payment gateways.
  • NETD4TE Premium Subscription: Become a premium member to unlock the ultimate experience. Benefits will include:
    • Verified Badge: Get a premium verified badge on your profile.
    • Ad-Free Experience: Claim all daily and emergency rewards instantly, without watching any ads.
    • Free Visitor Unlocks: See who has visited your profile without spending any currency.
    • Anonymous Browsing: Visit other profiles without them knowing you were there.

Stay tuned for these exciting updates!
